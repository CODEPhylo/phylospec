# Welcome to PhyloSpec Blog

We're excited to launch the PhyloSpec blog! This space will be dedicated to sharing:

- **Project updates** and milestone announcements
- **Technical deep-dives** into the modeling language and component library
- **Community highlights** from working group discussions
- **Tutorial content** for researchers and educators
- **Integration stories** from inference engine developers

## What's Next?

Stay tuned for upcoming posts about:

- The evolution of the PhyloSpec JSON schema
- Building your first PhyloSpec model
- Understanding the core component library
- Behind the scenes of the prototype implementations

We invite the community to contribute guest posts and share their experiences with PhyloSpec. If you're interested in writing for the blog, please reach out to the working group.

Happy modeling!
